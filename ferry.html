A Ferry Expedition

Discover Washington's Ferry Routes

Washington State Ferries provide stunning views of the Pacific Northwest and a unique way to see coastal areas.

  • Bainbridge Island Ferry: A scenic ride to a charming island town.
  • Vashon Island Ferry: A ride to an island with nature trails and small beaches.
  • Bremerton Ferry: A longer trip that has amazing coastal views and access to museums.
