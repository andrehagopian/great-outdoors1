Explore the Mist & Mountains

Discover Washington's Hiking Activities

Experience breathtaking mountain vistas to quiet coastal routes and vibrant city greenways, every trail invites you to immerse yourself in nature and adventure at your own pace. Trek through the rugged peaks of national parks, explore the serene forests of state trails, or stroll scenic urban paths, hiking in the Pacific Northwest offers an incredible variety of landscapes and experiences.

  • National Parks: Notable national parks within Washington state include, Mount Rainier National Park, Olympic National Park, and North Cascades National Park.
  • State Parks: Notable Washington state parks include, Deception Pass State Park, Lake Wenatchee State Park, and Palouse Falls State Park.
  • City Parks: Notable city parks within Washington include, Discovery Park Loop Trail, Dune Peninsula Trail, and Riverfront Park Trails.
